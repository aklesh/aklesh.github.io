20W Portable Solar Panel Review

During our last month-long expedition to the high plateau, keeping our communications equipment charged was a critical priority. We tested the SunCharge 20W panel to see if it could hold up against the harsh UV and sub-zero temperatures.

Durability

The panel features an ETFE coating which proved resistant to scratching, even when strapped to the outside of a pack while scrambling through scree.

Efficiency

In full sun at 4000m, we consistently saw 18W output, which is remarkably close to the rated maximum. In overcast conditions, this dropped to around 5W, which was still sufficient to trickle charge our satellite messenger.

Verdict

For expeditions longer than 5 days where weight is a concern, this panel earns its place in the pack. Sticky post test!

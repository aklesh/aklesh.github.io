Alpine Walker GT Review

Finding a boot that hikes well on the approach but climbs well on vertical ice is the holy grail. The Alpine Walker GT attempts to bridge this gap.

Comfort

Out of the box, the fit is narrow. Break-in time was substantialâ€”expect at least 30km of hiking before they feel compliant.

Climbing Performance

On vertical ice, the shank stiffness provides excellent support for front-pointing. The ankle flexibility is a bit limited for complex mixed climbing moves.

Verdict

A solid workhorse boot for general mountaineering, though specialized ice climbers might want something more technical.

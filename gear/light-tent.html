Summit Seeker 2P Review

Weight is everything when you have to carry 10 days of food. The Summit Seeker 2P claims a trail weight of just 1.1kg. We took it out for a week to verify those claims.

Setup

The single-pole design is intuitive and fast to pitch. In high winds, however, it requires perfect guy-line placement to maintain structure.

Condensation

Single-wall tents are notorious for condensation. This model manages it better than most thanks to aggressive venting at the peak and footbox.

Verdict

A fantastic shelter for fast-and-light summer objectives, but I wouldn’t trust it in a serious winter storm.

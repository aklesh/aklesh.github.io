A Visitor in Camp: Arctic Fox

üìÖ 2024-02-20 üìç Coordinate: -77.842, 166.666

A Lone Wanderer

We had a visitor today. A beautiful white arctic fox spent several hours investigating the perimeter of our storage tents.

He seemed entirely unfazed by our presence, watching us with a curiosity that matched our own. It’s amazing how such a small creature can thrive in this extreme environment. We kept our distance to ensure he didn’t get too accustomed to human food.

Seeing wildlife reaffirms that we are guests in this ecosystem. It’s a reminder to keep our footprint as light as possible.

He eventually padded off toward the ridge, leaving nothing but a set of small tracks in the fresh powder.

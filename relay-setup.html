Bridging the Gap

Connectivity is our lifeline. Today, we hauled a heavy relay station to the top of ‘Beacon Peak’ to ensure we have a solid signal between base camp and our southern survey teams.

The setup was difficult due to the wind and the steep, icy terrain, but the solar panels are now positioned to catch maximum sunlight during the long arctic day.

We successfully tested the data link by streaming a real-time feed from our highest sensors. This will dramatically speed up our data collection process during the final push.

Science is increasingly a digital endeavor, even at the ends of the Earth.
